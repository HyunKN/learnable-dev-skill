# Learning Playbook

## Goal

Use AI in a way that leaves behind technical understanding, not just finished code.

## Default Habits

- Ask what changed and why, not only for the final patch.
- Try to explain the structure again after implementation.
- Read the changed files at least once before considering the work learned.
- Keep a short note for meaningful features.

## Five Questions Per Technology

For any important tool or library, be able to answer:

1. What is it?
2. Why was it used?
3. What are its strengths?
4. What are its weaknesses?
5. How is it used in this project?
